Form View Full Screen/Width & Chatter Hide

This module adds a toggle button for chatter in form view on large screens

  1. Toggle the position of the chatter (messages & activities) to the side of the form view for better usability on wide monitors.
  2. Instantly hide or show the chatter panel to maximize workspace and focus on form content.
  3. Enable full-width (full screen) mode for form views, removing default width restrictions for a more immersive editing experience.
